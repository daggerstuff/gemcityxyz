## Philosophy

Technology should empower the marginalized, not entrench the powerful. Code is a tool of liberation when wielded with intention. AI and LLMs represent unprecedented potential — but only if we resist the urge to automate oppression.

This blog exists to explore that tension: the cutting edge of technology alongside critical analysis of its impact. No corporate fluff. No tech-bro hype. Just sharp thinking about where we're headed and whether we should be going there at all.
